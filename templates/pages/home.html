{% icon 'info' size=16 %} FAQ

Straight answers.

Where do I find the IMEI?

Dial *#06# on any phone, or look in Settings → General → About. It's also printed on the SIM tray and the original box.

How fast are results?

Most checks land in under a minute. A few registry lookups take a couple of minutes — we show a live status the whole time.

What if the phone is flagged?

You walk away before you pay. That's the whole point — a {{ BRAND.currency_symbol }}1.49 check beats a stolen phone you can never activate.

Do you work for every brand?

Yes — Apple, Samsung, Google, Xiaomi and more. The IMEI standard is universal; our sources cover the major carriers and registries worldwide.
