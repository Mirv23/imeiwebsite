{% icon 'info' size=16 %} FAQ

A few straight answers.

Where do I find the IMEI?

Dial *#06# on any phone, or look in Settings → General → About. It's also printed on the SIM tray and the original box.

How fast are results?

Most checks come back in under a minute. A few registry lookups take a little longer — we show a live status the whole time.

What if the phone is flagged?

Then you know before you pay, and you can walk away. That's the whole point — a small check is easier to absorb than a phone you can never activate.

Do you work for every brand?

The IMEI standard is universal, so we cover Apple, Samsung, Google, Xiaomi and more, drawing on the major carriers and registries.
